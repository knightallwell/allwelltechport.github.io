Algorithmic Trading with Deep Reinforcement Learning (PPO)

This project explores the implementation of Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL) for algorithmic trading, leveraging Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) to optimize trading strategies. The model is trained to interact with a simulated trading environment, making real-time buy, sell, or hold decisions based on historical market data.

Key Highlights:

• Agentic AI Integration: The model operates as an autonomous Agentic AI, continuously learning and adapting to market conditions. By leveraging Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO), the AI agent refines its trading strategy over time, improving its ability to maximize returns while mitigating risk.

• Deep Reinforcement Learning Framework: Utilized Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO), a state-of-the-art reinforcement learning algorithm, to train an intelligent trading agent.

• Market Data Integration: Incorporated financial market data, specifically focusing on the top ten companies listed in the S&P 500.

• Automated Data Updates: Users can simply run the "Top 10 Companies Listed on SP500 Mar/2025" notebook if they want the most recent market data. The code is designed to automatically fetch the latest market conditions, ensuring up-to-date insights.

• Action-Based Rewards: Designed a structured reward system to reinforce profitable trading decisions and penalize poor ones.

• Performance Evaluation: Assessed model performance using key financial metrics, including cumulative returns and Sharpe ratio.

• This project demonstrates the practical application of AI-driven trading strategies, enhancing decision-making in financial markets through reinforcement learning techniques.
